About Me

I am a systems-focused IT professional currently serving as an IT Officer at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU). With over three years of international experience at the Canada Revenue Agency (CRA), I have specialized in maintaining national-level data integrity and building robust digital infrastructures.

My transition into education is driven by a "Learning by Implementation" philosophy. I believe that Mathematics and ICT are most engaging when students can see the practical logic behind the theory—whether that is through Python automation, SQL database normalization, or statistical modeling.

Having navigated my own path from average academic results to achieving a Level 5* in DSE Mathematics through disciplined self-study, I am committed to mentoring students with empathy. I aim to help the next generation master the technical rigor required for their examinations while preparing them for the demands of a global digital future.

Tableau - Hong Kong Housing Affordability Dashboard

This interactive Tableau dashboard analyzes Hong Kong’s housing affordability using open data on median income and housing prices across districts. By visualizing the price-to-income ratio and mortgage burden over time, the dashboard reveals critical spatial and temporal disparities. It aims to inform equitable housing policies and stimulate discussion on how data transparency can empower both citizens and decision-makers in tackling the city’s long-standing affordability crisis.

#OpenData   #HousingPolicy   #DataVisualization   #UrbanInequality Learn more

Tableau - Hong Kong Urban Mobility Dashboard

Built with Tableau and powered by datasets from the Hong Kong Transport Department and MTR, this project maps multimodal transit access and last-mile connectivity across the city. It uncovers gaps in public transport equity, especially for lower-income and underserved districts. The dashboard offers policy suggestions to improve inclusive urban mobility and demonstrates how open data can be leveraged to support people-centered, evidence-based transport planning.
