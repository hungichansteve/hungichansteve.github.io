About Me

I’m a Computer Science graduate with 3 years of experience at the Canada Revenue Agency, where I worked as an IT Developer/Analyst in the Compliance division. I built data models and dashboards using IBM Cognos and Framework Manager to support internal policy and reporting needs.

After completing a postgraduate certificate in Business Intelligence System Infrastructure at Algonquin College, I became passionate about using data not just for business—but for better public decisions. I believe that behind every dataset is a story about people and the systems that serve them.

I’m currently preparing to transition into the field of public policy, where I aim to bridge technical skills and public service to make meaningful, data-driven impact.
